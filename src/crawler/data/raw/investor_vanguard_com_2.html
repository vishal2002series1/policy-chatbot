Frequently asked questions

The best option for you will depend on how much you intend to invest and the complexity of your financial management needs. Professional advice may be a good fit if you want a smart strategy, want help managing your investments, have questions about investing, and believe in the power of a long-term view.

Advice may not be a good fit if:

  • You have experience investing and want to take a more hands-on approach.
  • You have a basic saving need that you can manage on your own.
  • You don’t want to pay for investment management and monitoring services.

Our guide is a helpful resource for understanding which advisory service may be best for you. You also can contact one of our investment professionals with questions at 800-742-9998.

While we don't have 24/7 phone or chat services, you can access your account online anytime. Our investment professionals are available by phone to help with your questions Monday through Friday from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. Eastern time. Depending on the advice service you select, you also may have a dedicated advisor to contact.

The minimum amount you'll need to enroll depends on the advisory service you select:

  • Digital Advisor: $100
  • Personal Advisor: $50,000
  • Personal Advisor Select: $500,000
  • Personal Advisor Wealth Management: $5,000,000

Your costs may vary based on the service you select and how much you invest. The fee calculator on each advisory service page is a great way to estimate your costs.

Although we don't provide free investment advice, we do have helpful educational resources available to all investors at no cost.
